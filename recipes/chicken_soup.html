Recipe: Chicken Soup

Image of Chicken Soup

This is Chicken Soup, cheap, fast, delicious, and filling. It is the meal of choice for lazy perople on a budget.

It is also incredible easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 1 Potato
  • 1 to 2 Tomatoes
  • 1 to 2 Carrots
  • 1 Yellow Onion
  • 1 Chicken Thigh Drumstick
  • 400ml Water

Steps

  1. Defrost the Chicken Thigh Drumstick.
  2. Chop the Potatoes, Tomatoes, Carrots, and Yellow Onion into smaller pieces.
  3. Place contents into Rice Cooker.
  4. Add 400ml of Water.
  5. Turn on Rice Cooker, set to Soup mode.
  6. Wait an hour and a half for the process to complete.
  7. The meal is ready. Enjoy!
