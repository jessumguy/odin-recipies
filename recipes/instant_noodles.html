Recipe: Instant Noodles

The food of choice for poor college students.

Poor man's food. Cheap, tasty, and unhealthy.

Tip: Add an egg for additional nutrition.

Ingredients

  • 1 Packet Instant Noodles (Flavouring of your choice)
  • 1 Egg
  • Water

Steps

  1. Open Packet and place the noodle brick into Rice Cooker.
  2. Add water into cooker, the noodles should be submerged.
  3. Add flavour pack into cooker.
  4. Turn on Rice Cooker, set to Reheat.
  5. Wait 5 to 10 minutes for the water to boil, then crack Egg inside.
  6. Wait another 5 minutes to allow egg to be cooked.
  7. The meal is ready. Enjoy!
